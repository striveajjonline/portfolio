ABOUT ME

I'm Snekan & Poet Lyricist Actor Politician

Hi, My name is Snekan. I am a Tamil poet, politician, lyricist, playback singer, and actor with experience in Tamil cinema. I started my political career in 2018 with Makkal Needhi Maiam and ran for Lok Sabha. He ran for the Virugambakkam Constituency in the 2021 Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly election on behalf of Makkal Needhi Maiam.
